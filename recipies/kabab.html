Kabab

Description

Kashmiri seekh kebab is a flavorful and aromatic grilled dish made with minced meat and a blend of spices. The kebabs are traditionally cooked on skewers over a charcoal grill, giving them a smoky flavor. They are juicy, tender, and packed with the flavors of Kashmiri cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 500 gram Minced Meat.
  • 1 Onion, Grated.
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
  • 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chili Powder.
  • 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric Powder.
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garam Masala.
  • As required Salt To Taste.
  • As needed Skewers For Grilling.
